In the software community it's widely accepted that we are a means of life quality embetterment. With life's pace on the rise, the means by which we as humans get what we need, or think we need, must become equally quickly paced and readily accessible. As one of the more renowned John Peters puts it, complex but not complicated. We must strive to make our designs as complex as necessary, without skipping important details, yet presenting these complexities in a way that can be widely used, and widely accepted as an advancement.
